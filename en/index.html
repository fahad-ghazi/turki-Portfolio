About

"I make Riyadh as I see it in a dream."

Turki Alnefaie is a Saudi visual artist and storyteller based in Riyadh, working at the intersection of cinema and artificial intelligence. He builds visual worlds that channel Saudi culture and local memory using the latest generation of generative AI tools.

Since 2025, Turki has produced 9 short films, trained 8 character models at 5 angles each, and directed more than 290 editorial images for brands, real estate developers, and cultural content.

How I Work

Production methodology, not random prompts
  1. 01

    Reading

    I start from the cultural reference. The film isn't born from a prompt — it's born from a deep reading of Riyadh's scene, popular memory, and symbol.

  2. 02

    Script + Board

    Written script, sketched storyboard, moodboard for color and lighting. Every shot is defined before I open any tool.

  3. 03

    Character Training

    If the project needs a consistent character, I train a custom LoRA model on 40–60 images. The result: visual consistency across multiple angles.

  4. 04

    Generation + Editing

    I use Sora, Runway Gen-3, Midjourney, and ComfyUI workflows. Each shot is generated 20–40 times before selecting the keeper.

  5. 05

    Cut + Sound

    Edit in Premiere, color grading in DaVinci, sound design crafted scene by scene. A film is not delivered until the sound is sealed.
