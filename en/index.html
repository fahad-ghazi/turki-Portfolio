About

عن تركي

Turki Alnefaie is a Saudi visual artist based in Riyadh, working at the intersection of cinema and artificial intelligence. He produces short films, editorial imagery, advertising, and AI-built worlds that channel Saudi culture and local memory using the new generation of generative AI tools.

Turki collaborates with brands, real estate developers, and producers on projects spanning music videos, advertising campaigns, editorial fashion, and architectural visualization — with a cinematic visual language and a contemporary Saudi identity.
