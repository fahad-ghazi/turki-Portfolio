Designing with Purpose:

Lumora Beauty Rebrand

We transformed Lumora into a modern luxury skincare brand with refined visuals, sustainable packaging, and a conversion-focused eCommerce experience designed for growth.

Where The Journey Started


Every brand begins with a story. The Lumora journey started with a need to redefine how modern beauty feels and connects with people. The goal was to move beyond trends and create a brand that feels timeless, confident, and deeply aligned with its audience.



Building Brand Identity


Over time, the focus shifted toward crafting a strong visual identity including logo design, color systems, and brand voice. Each element was refined to reflect elegance, clarity, and consistency across all touchpoints, ensuring the brand communicates with purpose and style.



Working On Real Execution


Real execution brought the vision to life. From packaging design to digital presence, every detail was carefully considered. Collaborating with stakeholders, adapting to feedback, and iterating on ideas helped shape a cohesive and impactful brand experience.

Finding Inspiration


Inspiration came from a blend of beauty, culture, and minimal design principles. Observing how users interact with beauty products and brands helped guide decisions, turning simple ideas into meaningful and engaging visual narratives.



Evolving As A Brand


The brand continues to evolve with changing trends and user expectations. New ideas, tools, and strategies help refine its direction, ensuring Lumora stays relevant while maintaining its core identity and values.



Final Thoughts


For Lumora, rebranding was more than a visual update. It was about creating a refined experience that connects with people, builds trust, and delivers a sense of beauty that feels authentic, modern, and lasting.
